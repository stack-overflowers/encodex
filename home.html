How EncodeX Encryption Works

Our proprietary hybrid encryption system combines the best of both cryptographic worlds to secure your VoIP communications with minimal performance overhead.

Asymmetric encryption establishes secure connections and exchanges keys, while symmetric encryption protects the actual voice data with optimal efficiency.

EncodeX Encryption Process

1

Secure Transmission
