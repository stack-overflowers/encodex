About EncodeX

EncodeX is a military-grade encryption platform designed to protect your digital communications with cutting-edge cryptographic technology. Our systems utilize advanced algorithms to ensure your messages remain confidential and tamper-proof.

In an era of increasing digital surveillance, EncodeX provides a secure haven for your private communications. We implement zero-knowledge architecture, meaning even we cannot access your encrypted data.

Core Features

End-to-End Encryption

Messages are encrypted on your device before transmission and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient.

Biometric Authentication

Optional biometric verification adds an extra layer of security to your encrypted communications.

Self-Destructing Messages

Set expiration times for sensitive messages that automatically delete after reading.

Perfect Forward Secrecy

Each session uses unique encryption keys that are never reused, preventing mass decryption.

Security Protocol

EncodeX employs a multi-layered security architecture:

AES-256-GCM ECDH-521 SHA-3 RSA-4096 HMAC PBKDF2 OAuth 2.0 Zero-Knowledge
